About Me

I am a Senior AI Engineer and Agent Architect specializing in the design and deployment of production-grade autonomous systems. With over 15 years of engineering experience, I bridge the gap between advanced research and operational stability. My expertise lies in architecting multi-agent systems, optimizing RAG pipelines for high-precision retrieval, and scaling LLM platforms on AWS (Bedrock, SageMaker).

I focus on the entire lifecycle of AI systems—from Infrastructure-as-Code and MLOps to cost optimization and responsible AI governance. As an AWS Community Builder and 2X AWS Certified professional, I have a proven track record of delivering high-impact solutions across the IT, Telecommunications, and Consulting sectors.

I prioritize engineering reliability, observable architectures, and tangible business ROI over experimental demos. I am also an advocate for knowledge sharing through public speaking, volunteering, and my upcoming book, "Monetize the Machine."
